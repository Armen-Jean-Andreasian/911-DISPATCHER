Hurricanes

Preparing for a Hurricane:

Here are some important steps to take when preparing for a hurricane:

  • Stay informed about the hurricane's progress through local news and authorities.
  • Create an emergency kit with essential supplies, including food, water, medications, and batteries.
  • Secure your home by boarding up windows and reinforcing doors.
  • Trim trees and secure outdoor objects that could become projectiles in strong winds.
  • Develop an evacuation plan and identify evacuation routes.
  • Review your insurance coverage to ensure it includes hurricane-related damages.

During a Hurricane:

When a hurricane is approaching, follow these guidelines:

  • Stay indoors and away from windows and glass doors.
  • Listen to emergency alerts and follow the instructions provided by local authorities.
  • Avoid using candles for lighting, as they pose a fire hazard. Use battery-powered lights instead.
  • Keep your emergency kit and important documents easily accessible.
  • Do not venture outside until authorities declare it safe to do so.
