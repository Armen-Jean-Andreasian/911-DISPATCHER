Robbery

Emergency Response Tips:

  • If you are a victim of a robbery, try to stay calm and comply with the robber's demands to minimize the risk of harm.
  • Do not resist or confront the robber, as it can escalate the situation and increase the chances of violence.
  • Observe any distinguishing features of the robber, such as their appearance, clothing, or any unique identifiers.
  • Once the robber has left, contact the authorities immediately to report the incident.
  • Provide the police with detailed information about the incident and any relevant descriptions.
  • Try to preserve any evidence that may aid in the investigation, such as fingerprints or surveillance footage.
  • Seek support from friends, family, or professionals to cope with the emotional aftermath of the robbery.
