Heart Attacks

Symptoms of a Heart Attack:

Recognizing the symptoms of a heart attack is crucial for prompt medical intervention. Look out for the following signs:

  • Chest pain or discomfort, often described as pressure, squeezing, or fullness
  • Pain or discomfort in the arms, neck, jaw, or stomach
  • Shortness of breath
  • Nausea, vomiting, or lightheadedness
  • Cold sweat or clammy skin

Emergency Response for a Heart Attack:

If someone is experiencing a heart attack, follow these emergency response steps:

  1. Call 911 or your local emergency number immediately.
  2. Help the person sit or lie down in a comfortable position.
  3. If they are unconscious and not breathing, begin CPR.
  4. If an automated external defibrillator (AED) is available, follow the instructions to use it.
  5. Stay with the person until emergency medical services arrive.
