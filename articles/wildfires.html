Wildfires

Preparing for a Wildfire:

Here are some important steps to take when preparing for a wildfire:

  • Create a defensible space around your property by removing flammable materials.
  • Keep gutters, roofs, and vents clear of debris.
  • Have an emergency evacuation plan and practice it with your family.
  • Pack a "go bag" with essential items, including important documents, medications, and clothing.
  • Keep your vehicle fueled and ready for evacuation.
  • Stay informed about fire conditions and evacuation orders through local news and authorities.

During a Wildfire:

If you find yourself in the midst of a wildfire, follow these guidelines:

  • Evacuate immediately if instructed to do so by authorities.
  • Stay low to the ground and cover your nose and mouth with a damp cloth to reduce smoke inhalation.
  • Avoid areas with heavy smoke and seek shelter in a building if possible.
  • Do not attempt to fight the fire unless you have proper training and equipment.
  • Stay connected with emergency services and follow their instructions.
