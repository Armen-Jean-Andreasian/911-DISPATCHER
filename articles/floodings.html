Floodings

What to Do During a Flooding:

Here are some important steps to take during a flooding:

  • Stay informed about the current situation through local news and authorities.
  • If instructed to evacuate, do so immediately. Follow designated evacuation routes.
  • Avoid walking or driving through floodwaters.
  • Move to higher ground if you are in a low-lying area.
  • Avoid contact with floodwaters, as they may be contaminated.
  • Listen to emergency alerts and follow the instructions provided.

After the Flooding:

Once the flooding has subsided, follow these steps:

  • Return home only when authorities indicate it is safe to do so.
  • Be cautious of hazards such as damaged buildings, fallen power lines, or debris.
  • Document any damage to your property for insurance purposes.
  • Clean and disinfect your home if it has been affected by floodwaters.
  • Contact your local disaster relief services for assistance, if needed.
