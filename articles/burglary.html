Burglary

Preventing Burglaries:

  • Ensure all doors and windows are locked when leaving your home or property.
  • Install and use security systems, such as alarms, cameras, and motion sensor lights.
  • Keep valuables out of sight and securely stored.
  • Don't advertise your absence from home or share your travel plans on public platforms.
  • Get to know your neighbors and consider forming a neighborhood watch group.

What to Do During a Burglary:

  • If you suspect a burglary is in progress, prioritize your safety and the safety of others.
  • Leave the premises immediately if it is safe to do so.
  • Do not confront or attempt to apprehend the burglars.
  • Call emergency services and provide them with the necessary information.

After a Burglary:

  • Do not enter your home or property if you suspect the burglars are still present.
  • Wait for the authorities to arrive and secure the area.
  • Document any damages or stolen items for insurance purposes.
  • Contact your local police department to report the burglary.
  • Take steps to enhance the security of your home or property to prevent future burglaries.
